Japan

Land of the Rising Sun

Geography

Japan is an island country in East Asia, located in the northwest Pacific Ocean. It consists of four main islands: Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku, along with 6,848 smaller islands. The country spans 377,975 square kilometers and has a varied geography with mountains, forests, and coastlines.

Culture

Japanese culture is a blend of traditional values and modern innovations. From ancient temples and tea ceremonies to anime and cutting-edge technology, Japan offers a unique cultural experience. The country is known for its politeness, respect for elders, and strong work ethic.

Must-Visit Attractions

Mount Fuji

Japan's highest mountain and an active volcano, standing at 3,776 meters. It's considered one of Japan's three sacred mountains.

Kyoto Temples

Home to over 1,600 Buddhist temples and 400 Shinto shrines, Kyoto preserves Japan's ancient cultural heritage.

Tokyo Skytree

A broadcasting and observation tower in Tokyo that stands 634 meters tall, making it the tallest structure in Japan.

Hiroshima Peace Memorial

A UNESCO World Heritage Site dedicated to the legacy of Hiroshima as the first city to suffer atomic bombing.
